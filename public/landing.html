Search and Organize

Twitter is an awesome platform to discover new content. However discovering new content and organizing that information is not as simple as it seems . UDiscoverMe tries to solve that problem by indexing tweets along with any embedded links within the tweet to make it searchable.

Interest Mining

Twitter's Data is growing at exponential rate and so is the content getting shared by a user. UDiscoverMe helps you to visualize interests of a particular user, in doing so UdiscoverMe provides user and content recommendation for free.

Social Identity

Your social identity is dictated by the content you favorite or tweet . UDiscoverMe helps you with presenting your interest graph to other users by creating a landing page for you. All your tweets / favorited content is available through that website.

Getting Started

Here are few hints to get you started

  1. Start by favoriting/tweeting/retweeting interesting and useful content from people whom you follow. The more interesting content you share about a topic your rank in that particular topic goes up.
  2. The content you share will be present on the landing page for your profile.
  3. Control your landing page by moving not so important favorites/tweets off the page.
  4. Discover content from other users of UDiscoverMe and merge your their interest with yours or vice versa.
